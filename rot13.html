How to use:

Enter the text you want to be encoded or decoded into the first box, then select how many spaces you want the characters shifted (i.e, for Rot 13, you would want it to be shifted by 13 spaces).

If you know how many spaces encoded text has been shifted by, select this number in the drop down menu.

Your encoded/decoded text will appear in the second box after you have selected a shift number.
